About SJCC

SJCC partners with public and nonprofit organizations to build stronger systems, equitable policies, and data-informed strategies. We specialize in grant development, reentry program design, and learning modules for research and justice-focused institutions.

Services
We support organizations with funding strategies, logic models, budget narratives, and compliance documentation.
We provide narrative reviews, scoring rubrics, and mock panel feedback to strengthen applications and strategic alignment.
We design learning modules for justice, public health, and policy-focused teams. Options include LMS content, workshops, and toolkits.
Highlighted Projects
  • Reentry Program Toolkit: Developed a customizable toolkit for post-incarceration programs focused on workforce training and housing navigation.
  • Data Equity Dashboard: Built an interactive reporting system for nonprofits to track demographic outcomes and funding distribution.
  • Funded Proposal Library: Created a searchable archive of successful proposals, budget samples, and funder match guides.
