About SJCC

SJCC partners with public and nonprofit organizations to build stronger systems, equitable policies, and data-informed strategies. We specialize in grant development, reentry program design, and learning modules for research and justice-focused institutions.

Services
Funding strategies, logic models, budgets, compliance matrices.
Scoring rubrics, federal-style reviews, panel feedback simulations.
Modules, LMS builds, AI prompts, and interactive tools.
Plain-language policy summaries, MOU translation, redline reviews.
SMART outcomes, logic models, dashboards, reports.
Coalition building, systems maps, sector alignment strategies.
Prompt libraries, ethical AI policies, AI-powered workflows.
Ongoing support, coaching, cohort learning, meeting design.
Highlighted Projects
  • Reentry Toolkit: Full guide for local program design + evaluation.
  • Equity Dashboard: Data visualization for community metrics + funder goals.
  • Justice Training Series: LMS course for advocacy groups and nonprofits.
