About SJCC

SJCC partners with public and nonprofit organizations to build stronger systems, equitable policies, and data-informed strategies. We specialize in grant development, reentry program design, and learning modules for research and justice-focused institutions.

Services

Grant Development & Compliance Advising

Includes federal proposals, subaward guidance, indirect cost negotiation, and Cayuse routing support.

Grant Review & Peer Evaluation

Served as reviewer for DOJ, CDC, and peer foundation applications with compliance and impact analysis.

Custom Training Module Design

Built onboarding curricula in Canvas with automation, voiceover tools, and AI-supported navigation.

Contract Review & Policy Translation

Translated research MOUs, NDAs, and foundation contracts into internal guides and team workflows.

Data-Driven Evaluation Frameworks

Created logic models, tiered monitoring systems, and visualization dashboards for grantee reporting.

Cross-Sector Collaboration Strategy

Facilitated partnership plans between courts, universities, and nonprofits including shared deliverables.

AI-Integrated Strategy for Nonprofits

Used tools like Gamma, Mindsera, CodePen, and Claude to streamline nonprofit learning and proposals.

Technical Assistance & Stakeholder Training

Delivered grant training, facilitated restorative sessions, and guided teams through post-award systems.

Highlighted Projects

  • GPOA Capstone: Developed a tiered evaluation model and grantee support framework
  • Canvas Onboarding: Designed LMS-based training modules for research administrators
  • DOJ Peer Review: Reviewed and scored federal justice grant applications
  • 19th JDC/Natchez: Managed reentry funding and compliance systems across jurisdictions
