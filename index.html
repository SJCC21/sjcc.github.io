About SJCC

SJCC partners with public and nonprofit organizations to build stronger systems, equitable policies, and data-informed strategies. We specialize in grant development, reentry program design, and learning modules for research and justice-focused institutions.

Services

  • Grant Development & Compliance Advising
  • Grant Review & Peer Evaluation (DOJ/CDC/Federal)
  • Custom Training Module Design (Canvas, LMS, AI-enhanced)
  • Contract Review & Policy Translation
  • Data-Driven Evaluation Frameworks & Reporting
  • Cross-Sector Collaboration Strategy (Courts, Higher Ed, Community)
  • AI-Integrated Strategy for Nonprofits & Learning Systems
  • Technical Assistance & Stakeholder Training

Highlighted Projects

  • GPOA Capstone: Developed a tiered evaluation model and grantee support framework
  • Canvas Onboarding: Designed LMS-based training modules for research administrators
  • DOJ Peer Review: Reviewed and scored federal justice grant applications
  • 19th JDC/Natchez: Managed reentry funding and compliance systems across jurisdictions
