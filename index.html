About SJCC

SJCC partners with public and nonprofit organizations to build stronger systems, equitable policies, and data-informed strategies. We specialize in grant development, reentry program design, and learning modules for research and justice-focused institutions.

Services
Grant Development & Compliance Advising
Funding strategies, logic models, budgets, compliance matrices.
Grant Review & Federal Peer Evaluation
Scoring rubrics, federal-style reviews, panel feedback simulations.
Training Module Design (Canvas, LMS, AI-enhanced)
Modules, LMS builds, AI prompts, and interactive tools.
Contract Review & Policy Translation
Plain-language policy summaries, MOU translation, redline reviews.
Evaluation Frameworks & Dashboards
SMART outcomes, logic models, dashboards, reports.
Cross-Sector Strategy & Systems Building
Coalition building, systems maps, sector alignment strategies.
AI Tools for Nonprofit/Academic Teams
Prompt libraries, ethical AI policies, AI-powered workflows.
Technical Assistance & Stakeholder Facilitation
Ongoing support, coaching, cohort learning, meeting design.
Highlighted Projects
  • GPOA Capstone Evaluation Redesign: Created a tiered monitoring system, data visualization model, and grantee training supports.
  • 19th JDC & Natchez Cross-Jurisdictional Strategic Plan: Developed a multi-site implementation framework to support justice reform across city/state boundaries.
  • Equity-Focused Grantmaking Strategy: Designed a funder-focused rubric, funding equity heat map, and a scalable implementation toolkit.
